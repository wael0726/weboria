Frequently Asked
Questions

1. What costs should I expect and how does the payment work ?

Besides the website design, additional costs may include hosting fees, domain registration, ongoing maintenance, and any custom features you require. We guide you through all potential expenses to avoid surprises. Payment will be made in full at the start so that the development team can fully dedicate themselves to your website. Payment can be done via bank transfer or PayPal.

2. I already have a website, which plan should I choose?

You should use Website Redesign when upgrading your existing website, especially since many sites are several years old and don’t look good anymore. In these cases, a full redesign is often necessary to modernize your online presence.

3. Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Yes, all websites we build are fully responsive and optimized to look great on any device, including smartphones and tablets, ensuring the best user experience across platforms.

4. How long does it take for my website to be ready?

Project timelines vary based on scope, but typically a website is completed within 2 to 3 weeks. We keep you updated regularly throughout the process.

5. Can I follow up and ask questions during the website development?

Yes, you can easily communicate with our team throughout the project via email, Instagram, or text messages to ask questions and follow the progress.

6. What do I need to send to you, so you can start my project?

We will send you an electronic form to fill out with all the necessary information, including images, logos, colors, contact details (phone number, address, postal code, email), social media links, and any specific requests. You may also choose not to provide logos or color preferences and let us handle the branding and design choices for you.

7. What if I need extra design services?

We offer additional design services such as branding, logo creation, UI/UX enhancements, and marketing materials. Contact us for a personalized quote.

8. Why choose us over other web agencies?

Unlike many agencies that charge high prices for basic websites and impose monthly fees, we offer competitive pricing with only a one-time design cost. You can check our Work section to see all our completed projects and the high quality of our websites.
